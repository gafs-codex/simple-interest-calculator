# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this community a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Standards

Examples of positive behaviour include:

- Being respectful
- Accepting constructive criticism
- Supporting other contributors
- Using welcoming language

Examples of unacceptable behaviour include:

- Harassment
- Trolling
- Discrimination
- Personal attacks

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards.
